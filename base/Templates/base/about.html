About IDPCHAMPS

Our Mission: Save the displaced we believe every child deserves a future. In the Nigeria and around the world, we work every day to give children and adults alike who are displaced, a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. When crisis strikes and children are most vulnerable, we are always among the first to respond and the last to leave. We do whatever it takes for children - every day and in times of crisis – transforming their lives and the future we share.
